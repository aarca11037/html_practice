Codedex

After realizing I had to take the time to brush up on my fundamentals, I came across the Codedex website. I was immediately drawn by the similarities to a video game. Having played games for the majority of my life, I was very receptive to the design. I found their guides very helpful and especially intuitive. I began learning Python as well as Web Development. I have also viewed the projects listed to further my understanding of the topics. I genuinely find a lot of promise in the website and also enjoy the oppotunities available in the community. I believe I finally found a guide that suits my style of learning.



The Fellowship

In July of 2024, I was accepted into the Headstarter AI Fellowship. During my time, I was supposed to work on 5 projects to strengthen in my skills and grow my network. Sadly, I did not prepare accordingly. I had multiple roadblocks with availability and time management. As a result, I was unable to complete every assignment. Having reflected on my mistake, I am now working actively to take my job hunt seriously.
